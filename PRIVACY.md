# Privacy Policy — Good to note

**Effective date: 2026-07-11**

Good to note ("the app") is a personal expense-tracking app. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. The short version: **your financial data stays on your device. We do not have accounts, servers, analytics, or advertising, and we never collect your data.**

## 1. We do not collect your data
The developer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or financial information from this app. There is no account and no login — the app has no way to identify you.

## 2. Where your data lives
Everything you enter — transactions, amounts, notes, categories, recurring rules, merchant mappings, message-parsing templates, and settings such as your base currency — is stored **locally on your device** in the app's on-device database. It is not uploaded anywhere and is not accessible to the developer.

## 3. The only network connection: exchange rates
When you record or convert an amount in a currency other than your base currency, and today's rate is not already cached on your device, the app makes a single secure (HTTPS) request to a public exchange-rate service, **open.er-api.com**, to fetch the current rates.

- The request contains **only a three-letter currency code** (for example, `SGD`) in the web address. That is all.
- It does **not** send your transactions, amounts, notes, merchants, categories, message text, any identifier, or any personal information — the request has no way to carry them.
- The rates returned are public and identical for every user. They are cached **locally on your device** so the app works offline and does not repeatedly ask.
- The app uses no API key and creates no account with that service.

Your use of that service is also subject to its own terms; the app sends it no information about you.

## 4. No analytics, no advertising, no tracking
The app contains **no analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking software**, and **no third-party SDKs** of any kind. Nothing about how you use the app is recorded or transmitted. The app does not track you across other apps or websites.

## 5. Permissions
The app requests **no** device permissions — no location, contacts, camera, photos, microphone, or notifications.

## 6. Backups and your control of your data
The app automatically saves a backup file of your database inside its own storage area on your device, and makes that file available to you through the iOS **Files** app (under **On My iPhone ▸ Good to note ▸ Backups**). This backup **stays on your device** unless **you** choose to copy it elsewhere (for example, to iCloud Drive or your computer). The app never uploads it. You can restore from a backup file you select, and you can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app.

## 7. Data retention and deletion
Because your data lives only on your device, **you** control retention. Deleting a transaction removes it from the local database; deleting the app removes all app data from your device. The developer holds no copy and therefore cannot access, retain, or delete data on your behalf.

## 8. Children's privacy
The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone, including children under 13. Because the app collects no personal information at all, it collects none from children.

## 9. Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

# 隐私政策 — Good to note

**生效日期:2026-07-11**

Good to note(“本应用”)是一款个人记账应用。本政策说明本应用会与不会如何处理你的信息。一句话概括:**你的财务数据只保存在你的设备上。我们没有账号、没有服务器、没有分析统计、没有广告,也绝不收集你的数据。**

## 1. 我们不收集你的数据
开发者不会收集、传输、出售或分享本应用中的任何个人或财务信息。应用没有账号、无需登录——本应用无法识别你的身份。

## 2. 你的数据保存在哪里
你录入的一切——交易、金额、备注、分类、周期规则、商户映射、短信/邮件解析模版,以及本位币等设置——都**本地保存在你设备上**应用自带的本地数据库中。这些数据不会被上传到任何地方,开发者也无法访问。

## 3. 唯一的联网:获取汇率
当你以非本位币录入或换算金额,且当天汇率尚未缓存在设备上时,本应用会向公开汇率服务 **open.er-api.com** 发起一次安全(HTTPS)请求,以获取当前汇率。

- 该请求的网址中**只包含一个三位货币代码**(例如 `SGD`),仅此而已。
- 它**不会**发送你的交易、金额、备注、商户、分类、短信/邮件文本、任何标识符或任何个人信息——该请求在技术上也无从携带这些内容。
- 返回的汇率是公开的,对所有用户都相同。汇率会**缓存在你本地设备上**,以便离线可用,并避免重复请求。
- 本应用不使用任何 API 密钥,也不会在该服务上创建账号。

你对该服务的使用同时受其自身条款约束;本应用不会向其发送任何关于你的信息。

## 4. 无分析、无广告、无追踪
本应用**不含任何分析统计、崩溃上报、广告或追踪组件**,也**不含任何第三方 SDK**。你如何使用本应用不会被记录或传输。本应用不会跨其他应用或网站追踪你。

## 5. 权限
本应用**不请求任何**设备权限——不涉及定位、通讯录、相机、相册、麦克风或通知。

## 6. 备份与你对数据的掌控
本应用会在你设备上其自身的存储区域内自动保存一份数据库备份文件,并通过 iOS**「文件」**应用向你开放(位于**「我的 iPhone ▸ Good to note ▸ Backups」**)。该备份**始终留在你的设备上**,除非**你自己**选择将它拷贝到别处(例如 iCloud 云盘或电脑)。本应用绝不会上传它。你可以从自己选择的备份文件中恢复,也可以随时通过删除应用来清除全部数据。

## 7. 数据留存与删除
由于你的数据只存在于你的设备上,**留存由你掌控**。删除一笔交易即会将其从本地数据库移除;删除应用即会清除设备上的全部应用数据。开发者不持有任何副本,因此无法代你访问、留存或删除数据。

## 8. 儿童隐私
本应用并非面向儿童,且不收集任何人(包括 13 岁以下儿童)的数据。由于本应用完全不收集个人信息,自然也不会收集儿童的信息。

## 9. 本政策的变更
若本政策发生变更,更新版本将以新的生效日期发布于本网址。

## 10. 联系方式
如对隐私有任何疑问,请联系:**[contact email]**
